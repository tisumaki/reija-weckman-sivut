Services

Finland, like many other small countries, is a peculiar market that requires special expertise due to its unique language and culture. If your business or organization is planning to sell a product, service or idea to the well educated and highly critical Finns, you’re going to need a hand.

The good news is I’m here to help. Take a look at these examples of what my trained, creative and essentially Finnish brain can do for you:

  • I can translate, localize or transcreate your website, software or product to the sort of Finnish that fits your company’s vision and target audience. You can determine the specs yourself or include me in the planning process.

  • I proofread the work of others to make sure they’ve got it right. It’s always good to have more than just one pair of human eyes to go through the same content.

  • Speaking of humans, I can help create your Finnish AI training data. Maybe one day your customer service bot will know Finnish better than me.

  • If you have a special project ahead and need consulting on the Finnish market, its segmentation possibilities and the ways to win over said segments, I can provide all this plus the indispensable linguistic planning.


How does that sound? To learn more, please keep scrolling or simply contact me.

About me

I was born, raised and educated in Finland and for the past four years I’ve been building my career as a language specialist with a strong orientation in tech and sales. In 2021 I decided to go full-time offering high-quality translation and language services, and I take special pride in my excellent stats in both accuracy and speed.

My working languages are English and Spanish; I have a Master’s Degree in Spanish Language and Cambridge certified C2 level in English. During my years at the University of Turku I did minor studies in Economic Sociology and focused on language use in digital consumerism in my master's thesis.

My current customer portfolio consists mainly of software, application, digital product and online sales companies based in Europe and North America. I’d be happy to hear more about your company and its plans for Finland.

Oh, and did I mention my extensive work experience in customer service and sales? It’s quite something. I’ve worked in both B2C and B2B markets in both Finland and Spain. Since I was 18 I’ve worked with an insane number of products and services, from tourism, education and retail all the way to food and telecommunications. So if your company does any of the above, you’re in luck.
